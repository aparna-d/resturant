    About

    Multi Cuisine Restaurant showcases inventive Indian cuisine by complementing the flavours and traditions of India with global ingredients , authenticity and techniques. It has been awarded the San Pellegrino ‘Best Restaurant in India’ by Asia’s 50 Best Restaurants for seven consecutive years (from 2015-2021).
